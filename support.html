Frequently asked questions

How do I follow live scores?

Open Kick Score and browse today's fixtures from the home screen. Tap any match for lineups, stats, and events. Pull down to refresh for the latest scores.

How does Following and match notifications work?

Follow a match from the home screen to save it to Following. When prompted, allow notifications so Kick Score can alert you before kickoff. You can manage this anytime in Settings → Notifications → Kick Score.

Why can't Kick Score send notifications?

Go to Settings → Notifications → Kick Score and enable Allow Notifications. Also confirm you have starred at least one upcoming match inside the app.

Scores or data look incorrect

Match data is supplied by third-party sports data providers and may occasionally be delayed or corrected after official confirmation. Pull to refresh in the app to fetch the latest update.

Is Kick Score affiliated with FIFA or any club?

No. Kick Score is an independent fan app. We are not affiliated with FIFA, national federations, clubs, or leagues. Logos and names are used for identification purposes only.

How do I cancel Kick Score Pro?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. On your iPhone: Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions → Kick Score → Cancel Subscription. Cancel at least 24 hours before renewal to avoid the next charge.

How do I restore my purchase?

On the subscription screen in the App or in Settings, tap Restore. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

What does the 3-day free trial include?

Eligible new subscribers receive Kick Score Pro access for three days. If you do not cancel before the trial ends, Apple automatically bills the weekly subscription shown on the paywall.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds are processed by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com and sign in with your Apple ID.

Still need help?

Include your iOS version, App version, and a short description of the issue when you email us.
